How To Use Dx2 Team Builder
Instructions:
  1. Select your first demon and the archetype you want them to have.
  2. Select their two inherited skills.
  3. Click the Brands option and assign the Brands for this demon at the bottom.
  4. Optional: Click Stats, Brands, or Mitama buttons above the demon to configure additional details about the demon.
  5. Repeat this process for the remaining 3 demons.
  6. Pick a liberator on the far right.
  7. Clicking 'Generate Link' will generate a unique URL to the build you just created.
  8. Clicking 'Reset' will reset the tool to its default state for you to build something else.
  9. For many 5* Demons you can Hover Over the demon/achetype image to get some additional details such as panel info or how you might use that demon.
FAQ:
  • Where can I Thank, Ask Questions, or Provide Feedback for this tool?
    • Easiest place to do that would be on Reddit. You can find me at: /u/AlenaelReal
  • Do things like Speedster, Agility Amp, Speed Brands, Panels, and Liberator's extra Agility/Speed % affect Total Party Speed?
    • Yes.
  • Do skills Agility Amp, Strength Amp, etc. affect the Demons Stats?
    • No they do not. Except Agi Amp bonuses they will be added to ensure we calculate turn order properly. The rest are ignored (Not added) to the demons stats.
  • Do stats from Liberators affect the partys overall stats?
    • Yes.
  • Do things like Lead/Lead x2 brands affect turn order?
    • Yes.
  • Does editing Speed % in Brands affect team speed?
    • Yes.
  • Does Panels that affect Speed % get calculated?
    • Yes, if you have the 'Add Panel Speed %' Checkbox selected.
